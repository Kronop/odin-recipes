Chicken Breasts

Description

These honey-mustard baked chicken breasts are super easy, delicious, and healthy! What's not to like?

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • Â½ cup honey
  • Â½ cup Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 4 (4 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray a baking dish with nonstick spray.
  2. Mix honey and mustard together in a bowl. Place bread crumbs in a second bowl.
  3. Roll chicken in honey-mustard, then roll in bread crumbs. Place in the prepared baking dish. Mix remaining honey-mustard and bread crumbs together and set aside.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Spoon reserved honey-mustard-crumb mixture over the chicken breasts and bake until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 10 to 15 more minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
